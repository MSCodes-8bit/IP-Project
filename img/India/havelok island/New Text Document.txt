It is not geographically correct to add this place to the list of cities. But, it is wrong to miss this island which is the largest one of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Splendid waters, lush vegetation and warm white sand makes this island a wonderful place to visit. You can enjoy all kinds of water related activities like snorkelling, diving and others. Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, Neil Island, Mount Harriet, Mahatma Gandhi Marine Park, Andaman Bubbles and many other places are located in this Island that attracts millions of people every year
